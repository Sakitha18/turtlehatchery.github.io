Welcome to the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

Discover the wonders of sea turtle conservation at the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery in Sri Lanka. This sanctuary is dedicated to the protection and preservation of endangered sea turtles. Explore the hatchery and learn about the nesting process as female turtles lay their eggs on the sandy shores. Witness the hatching of baby turtles in the incubation area and marvel at their journey into the ocean. Knowledgeable staff members are available to provide insights into these magnificent creatures and their conservation efforts. Immerse yourself in this enchanting experience that supports the survival of sea turtles.
